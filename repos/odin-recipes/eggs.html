Feta Eggs

A very TASTY way to add zip to boring scrambled eggs.

One of my favorite ways to cook eggs, there is a lot of others options but give this one a try.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • ¼ cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Saute onions until translucent.
  3. Pour in eggs.
  4. Cook, stirring occasionally to scramble.
  5. When eggs appear almost done, stir in chopped tomatoes and feta cheese, and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook until cheese is melted.
